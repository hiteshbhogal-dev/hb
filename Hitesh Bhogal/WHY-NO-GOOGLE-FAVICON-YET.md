## 🎯 **Why the Delay?**

Google doesn't show favicons immediately because:

1. **Google needs to crawl your site** (happens every few days)
2. **Google validates the favicon** (checks size, format, quality)
3. **Google caches the favicon** (adds to their CDN)
4. **Google updates search results** (rolls out gradually)

**This process takes 1-2 weeks** for most sites!
