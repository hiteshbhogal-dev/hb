# 📋 Google Forms Contact Setup Guide

## ✅ Your contact form is ready - just need to connect it to Google Forms!

## 🚀 **Step-by-Step Setup (5 minutes)**

### **Step 1: Create Google Form**

1. Go to **https://forms.google.com**
2. Click **"+ Blank"** to create new form
3. Title it: **"Portfolio Contact Form - Hitesh Bhogal"**

---

### **Step 2: Add Form Fields**

Add these 4 questions **in this exact order**:

#### Question 1: Name
- Type: **Short answer**
- Question: **"Your Name"**
- Toggle: **Required** (ON)

#### Question 2: Email
- Type: **Short answer**
- Question: **"Your Email"**
- Toggle: **Required** (ON)
- Click ⋮ (three dots) → **Response validation**
  - Choose: **Text** → **Email**

#### Question 3: Subject
- Type: **Short answer**
- Question: **"Subject"**
- Toggle: **Required** (ON)

#### Question 4: Message
- Type: **Paragraph**
- Question: **"Your Message"**
- Toggle: **Required** (ON)

---

### **Step 3: Configure Form Settings**

1. Click **Settings** ⚙️ (top right)
2. Under **Responses** tab:
   - ✅ Check: **"Collect email addresses"**
   - ✅ Check: **"Send me email notifications for new responses"**
   - ✅ Check: **"Limit to 1 response"** (optional)
3. Under **Presentation** tab:
   - Confirmation message: **"Thank you! I'll get back to you soon."**
4. Click **Save**

---

### **Step 4: Get Form IDs**

1. Click **Send** button (top right)
2. Click **< >** (link/embed icon)
3. Copy the entire URL that looks like:
   ```
   https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSc...../viewform
   ```

4. **Get Entry IDs:**
   - Right-click on form → **Inspect** (or F12)
   - Click on **Name** field
   - In the code, look for: `name="entry.XXXXXXXXX"`
   - Copy that number (e.g., `entry.123456789`)
   - Repeat for Email, Subject, and Message fields

You'll have 4 entry IDs like:
- Name: `entry.123456789`
- Email: `entry.987654321`
- Subject: `entry.555555555`
- Message: `entry.777777777`

---
